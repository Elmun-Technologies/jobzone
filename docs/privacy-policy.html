Yolla — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 June 2026

About Yolla. Yolla is a mobile job-search and recruitment marketplace for Uzbekistan, connecting job seekers and employers. This policy explains what we collect, why, who we share it with, and your choices. Operator: Elmun Technologies. 2. How we use your information

  • Operate the marketplace: post jobs, search, apply, manage applicants, chat.
  • Match seekers and jobs (including an optional AI "good match" assessment).
  • Send notifications you've enabled (in-app, push, and — if you connect it — Telegram).
  • Keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Visibility to other users

  • When you mark yourself "open to work," employers can find your public profile card (name, headline, photo, city, skills).
  • Your exact home coordinates, phone, email, bio and home address are not shown to other users; precise applicant location is shared with an employer only for the specific job you applied to.
  • Employers' job postings and company profiles are public within the app.

4. Service providers we share data with

We use trusted processors who handle data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication, database and file storage (your account, profile, applications, resumes, messages).
  • Meilisearch — search index for public job listings.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivering push notifications (device token).
  • Yandex MapKit — map display; may process device/location data per Yandex's policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — optional AI features (job-post drafting, match assessment); the relevant job text and your listed skills are sent for processing when you use those features.
  • Telegram — only if you connect Telegram for notifications; we then send notification text to your linked chat.

5. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to meet legal obligations.

6. Your rights & choices

  • Access, correct or update your profile data in the app at any time.
  • Control notification permissions in the app and your device settings.
  • Control location sharing via your device permissions.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data by emailing us (see Contact).

7. Children

Yolla is intended for users aged 16 and older and is not directed at children.

8. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access to personal data is restricted by row-level security on our backend. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we'll revise the "Effective date" above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
